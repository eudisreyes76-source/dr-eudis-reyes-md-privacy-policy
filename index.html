Privacy Policy — FODMAP Cookbook Marketing Automation

Last updated: June 13, 2026

This privacy policy describes how the “FODMAP Cookbook Marketing Automation” application (“the App”) accesses and uses information from Pinterest on behalf of its owner, Dr. Eudis Reyes, MD (“the Owner”).

1. Who operates this App

This App is a personal, single-account automation tool operated by Dr. Eudis Reyes, MD, author of The Doctor's Low-FODMAP Cookbook for Beginners, for the sole purpose of managing the Owner's own Pinterest content (pins and boards) related to this book.

Contact: eudis.reyes76@gmail.com

2. Information We Access

Through Pinterest's official API and OAuth authorization, the App may access:

  • Basic Pinterest account information (account ID, username, profile details)
  • Boards owned by the account
  • Pins created, updated, or read on the account's boards
  • Basic analytics/metrics for pins created by the account (impressions, clicks, saves)

3. How We Use This Information

The information accessed is used exclusively to:

  • Create, update, and schedule pins on the Owner's own Pinterest boards
  • Read existing boards and pins to avoid duplicates and organize content
  • Retrieve performance metrics for the Owner's own pins for reporting purposes

The App does not access, collect, or process information belonging to any other Pinterest user or account.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share any data accessed through this App with third parties. Data is used solely for the Owner's own marketing activities described above.

5. Data Storage and Security

Access tokens and any data retrieved via the API are stored securely and used only by the Owner's automation tools. Tokens can be revoked at any time by the Owner through Pinterest's account settings (Apps and Permissions).

6. Data Retention

Authentication tokens are retained only as long as needed to operate the automation described above, or until revoked by the Owner. Pin performance data retrieved for reporting is retained only for internal review purposes.

7. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated periodically. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

8. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact: eudis.reyes76@gmail.com
